How to use

Winner picks

After Victory the winner chooses what to serve. Could be classic. Could be vengeance.

Minimum bar

Most recipes use 3–4 ingredients. Stock vodka, whiskey, tequila, lime, sugar, and salt.

Sober table?

Swap the spirit for cold tea, coffee, or lemonade. Same color, same drama, less wrecking.

// the classics

Cocktails

Ten classics every bar should know. Perfect when the winner feels generous. All recipes serve one.

№ 01 · GIN-BASED
Negroni
Ingredients
30 ml Gin
30 ml Campari
30 ml Sweet vermouth
1 slice Orange
Method

Build in a rocks glass over ice. Stir for 15 seconds. Garnish with orange. Done.

№ 02 · WHISKEY-BASED
Whiskey Sour
Ingredients
50 ml Bourbon
25 ml Lemon juice
15 ml Simple syrup
opt. One egg white
Method

Dry-shake all ingredients 10 sec. Add ice, shake 10 more. Strain over fresh ice.

№ 03 · TEQUILA-BASED
Margarita
Ingredients
50 ml Blanco tequila
25 ml Triple sec / Cointreau
20 ml Lime juice
pinch Salt for the rim
Method

Rim the glass with lime and salt. Shake everything with ice for 15 sec. Strain.

№ 04 · VODKA-BASED
Moscow Mule
Ingredients
50 ml Vodka
15 ml Lime juice
120 ml Ginger beer
1 wedge Lime + mint
Method

Copper mug or any glass. Ice → vodka → lime → ginger beer. Stir. Mint on top.

№ 05 · RUM-BASED
Mojito
Ingredients
50 ml White rum
25 ml Lime juice
2 tsp Sugar
8–10 Mint leaves
~80 ml Soda water
Method

Muddle mint with sugar and lime (gentle — don't crush). Ice → rum → soda. Stir.

№ 06 · WHISKEY-BASED
Old Fashioned
Ingredients
60 ml Bourbon or rye whiskey
1 cube Sugar
2–3 dash Angostura bitters
1 peel Orange
Method

Muddle sugar, bitters, and splash of water. Big ice cube → whiskey → stir. Express orange peel over top.

№ 07 · LOW-ABV
Aperol Spritz
Ingredients
60 ml Aperol
90 ml Prosecco
30 ml Soda water
1 slice Orange
Method

Fill a wine glass with ice. Build 3-2-1 ratio: Prosecco → Aperol → soda. Stir gently. Orange slice.

№ 08 · VODKA-BASED
Espresso Martini
Ingredients
50 ml Vodka
30 ml Fresh espresso (cooled)
20 ml Kahlúa
10 ml Simple syrup
Method

Shake hard with ice for 20 sec — you want the foam. Double strain into a chilled coupe. Three coffee beans on top.

№ 09 · MULTI-SPIRIT
Long Island
Ingredients
15 ml Vodka
15 ml Rum
15 ml Tequila
15 ml Gin
15 ml Triple sec
25 ml Lemon juice
top Cola
Method

All spirits + lemon → ice → shake briefly. Pour into highball, top with cola. Lemon wedge. Drink slowly. We warned you.

№ 10 · BRUNCH OPTION
Bloody Mary
Ingredients
50 ml Vodka
120 ml Tomato juice
10 ml Lemon juice
2 dash Worcestershire sauce
2 dash Tabasco
pinch Salt + black pepper
1 stalk Celery
Method

Build in a highball over ice. Stir — never shake (tomato foams ugly). Celery stalk. Best served morning-after.

// fast and loud

Shots

Ten classic shots. One gulp, no prep. Perfect for Headshot, Ratatat, Duel, and Roulette cards.

№ 11
Tequila Slammer
Ingredients
40 ml Blanco tequila
1 wedge Lime
pinch Salt
Method

Salt on the back of the hand. Lick — shoot — bite the lime. Classic. No variations.

№ 12
B-52
Layered ingredients
15 ml Kahlúa (bottom)
15 ml Baileys (middle)
15 ml Grand Marnier (top)
Method

Layer each spirit over the back of a spoon. Optional: ignite the top → wait 3 sec → blow out → shoot.

№ 13
Kamikaze
Ingredients
20 ml Vodka
20 ml Triple sec
10 ml Lime juice
Method

Shake with ice 10 sec. Strain into a shot glass. Down it. Forget it ever happened.

№ 14
Jägerbomb
Ingredients
30 ml Jägermeister
~100 ml Energy drink
Method

Drop the Jäger shot into a glass of energy drink. Drink immediately while it fizzes.

№ 15
Lemon Drop
Ingredients
30 ml Vodka
1 wedge Lemon
1 tsp Sugar
Method

Dip the lemon wedge in sugar. Shoot the vodka. Bite the lemon. Sugar softens the burn.

№ 16
Flaming Sambuca
Ingredients
30 ml Sambuca
2–3 Coffee beans
Method

Drop coffee beans into the shot. Ignite → wait 5 sec → blow out → drink in one gulp.

№ 17
Irish Slammer
Ingredients
15 ml Baileys
15 ml Jameson
150 ml Guinness (half pint)
Method

Layer Baileys + Jameson in a shot glass. Drop the shot into the Guinness. Chug fast — if Baileys curdles, you lose.

№ 18
Vodka Bomb
Ingredients
30 ml Vodka
~100 ml Energy drink
Method

Drop the vodka shot into the energy drink. Slam it. Like Jägerbomb, no taste, fast effect.

№ 19
Mexican
Ingredients
30 ml Blanco tequila
2 dash Tabasco
1 wedge Lime
Method

Drop Tabasco into the tequila. Shoot. Bite the lime. Spicy version of the classic slammer.

№ 20
Snake Bite
Ingredients
15 ml Jägermeister
15 ml Blanco tequila
Method

Pour Jäger first, then tequila on top. Don't stir. Shoot fast. Two bites — bitter then burn.

// vengeance

Tactical Disasters

CAUTION · DISASTER RULES
  • · All ingredients are regular food. Spicy, salty, bitter — it's about the taste, not your health.
  • · Never use: vinegar, pure alcohol above 40%, undiluted hot sauces in large doses, or any non-food liquid.
  • · If anyone has gastritis, allergies, diabetes, or is pregnant — skip the disasters, play the classics.
  • · It's a joke. If anyone's actually suffering, game over for everyone.

Ten recipes designed for the losers. Each named after a card from the game. The winner picks the victim and the punishment.

№ 21 · FROM THE MINE CARD
Mine Detonator
SHOT
Ingredients
30 ml Vodka
1 tsp Sriracha
pinch Black pepper
Method

Mix in a shot glass. Drink in one gulp. In 5 seconds you'll know why it's called Mine.

№ 22 · FROM THE TRAP CARD
Trap Special
SHOT
Ingredients
30 ml Tequila
1 tsp Pickle brine
1 wedge Lime
Method

Pour tequila. Top with pickle brine (don't stir). Lime on the side. Looks normal — until it isn't.

№ 23 · FROM THE ROULETTE CARD
Russian Roulette
GROUP
Ingredients (6 shots)
5× Plain vodka shots
1× Cold strong black coffee
Method

Line up 6 shot glasses. Five vodka, one coffee — they look identical. Each player picks a random shot. One loser.

№ 24 · FROM THE HOOK CARD
Friendly Fire
COCKTAIL
Ingredients
50 ml Warm beer
20 ml Whiskey
1 tsp Ketchup
1 piece Pickled cucumber
Method

Mix beer and whiskey (no ice). Add the pickle. Squirt ketchup on top. Serve with a straight face.

№ 25 · FROM THE DESERTER CARD
Deserter's Tonic
COCKTAIL
Ingredients
50 ml Gin
100 ml Tonic water
1 tsp Instant coffee (dry)
pinch Salt
Method

Make a regular G&T. Dust dry coffee and salt on top. Don't stir. Drink through a straw.

№ 26 · FROM THE RATATAT CARD
Ratatat Marathon
3× SHOT
Ingredients (3 shots)
1× Tequila shot
1× Pickle brine shot
1× Room-temperature beer shot
Method

Line up three shots. Drink all three in a row, no pause. No chaser allowed. Hold your breath.

№ 27 · FROM THE BALLAST CARD
Ballast Bomb
SHOT
Ingredients
30 ml Whiskey
1 tsp Soy sauce
1 tbsp Tomato juice
Method

Pour whiskey. Drop soy sauce and tomato juice into the shot. Looks heavy. Tastes worse. Down it.

№ 28 · FROM THE TIMEOUT CARD
Timeout Special
COCKTAIL
Ingredients
50 ml Vodka
100 ml Lukewarm milk
1 tsp Sugar
pinch Black pepper
Method

Mix vodka with warm milk and sugar. Pepper on top. The texture is the punishment. Drink slowly — there's no rush.

№ 29 · FROM THE AROUND THE WORLD CARD
Around the World
GROUP
Ingredients (per player)
15 ml Vodka (Russia)
15 ml Tequila (Mexico)
15 ml Whiskey (Ireland)
15 ml Sake (Japan)
Method

Four small shots in front of each player. Drink in clockwise order, one per turn. Last player standing wins respect.

№ 30 · FROM THE SNIPER'S NEST TOKEN
Sniper's Reward
COCKTAIL
Ingredients
40 ml Bourbon
20 ml Honey syrup
15 ml Lemon juice
1 dash Orange bitters
Method

For the player who emptied their reserve. Shake with ice, strain into a coupe. Smooth, golden, earned. Not a disaster — a reward.

// supply chain

Bar essentials

Minimum kit to cover 90% of these recipes. All available at any decent supermarket.

Spirits
  • · Vodka — neutral, any brand
  • · Blanco tequila — Margarita, Slammer
  • · Whiskey / bourbon — Old Fashioned, Sour
  • · White rum — Mojito
  • · Gin — Negroni, G&T
  • · Campari + sweet vermouth — for the Negroni
  • · Triple sec / Cointreau — orange utility liqueur
  • · Aperol — for the Spritz
  • · Kahlúa, Baileys, Jägermeister — for shots and bombs
Fresh & pantry
  • · Lime, lemon — base of all sours
  • · Orange — peels and garnish
  • · Sugar + simple syrup — balances acid
  • · Salt — Margarita rim, Bloody Mary
  • · Mint — Mojito
  • · Angostura bitters — Old Fashioned
  • · Tonic, soda, ginger beer, cola — base mixers
  • · Tomato juice + Worcestershire + Tabasco — Bloody Mary kit
Tools
  • · Shaker — Boston or 3-piece
  • · Jigger 15/30 ml — for measuring
  • · Bar spoon — long, twisted handle
  • · Strainer — Hawthorne or fine mesh
  • · Muddler — for mint and sugar
  • · Ice — lots of it, always fresh
Glassware
  • · Rocks (Old Fashioned) — low and wide, ~270 ml
  • · Highball — tall, ~350 ml
  • · Coupe / Martini — stemmed, no ice
  • · Shot glasses — 30–50 ml
  • · Copper mug — for Moscow Mule (optional)
  • · Wine glass — for Aperol Spritz
Tip from Drunken Arsenal

If your kit includes the Drunken Arsenal Bar Kit (jigger, strainer, spoon, muddler) — you're already covered for tools. Just stock the booze and grab a rocks glass (also included in the case).
